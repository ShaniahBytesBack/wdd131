Dance Diaries - Site Plan

This document outlines the structure, purpose, and design plans for the Dance Diaries website project.

Site Name

Name: Dance Diaries

Reason for Choosing: The name "Dance Diaries" reflects the website’s goal of documenting personal dance journeys. The term "Diaries" emphasizes personal growth, expression, and progress. It connects with the audience by suggesting an interactive, evolving platform that inspires dancers to record their growth and share experiences.

Suggested Domain: www.dancediaries.com (Available for purchase)

Site Purpose

The website is designed to provide dance enthusiasts of all levels with a resource hub to learn, track progress, and engage with a supportive community. The main goals of the site are to:

  • Provide tutorials and resources: Offering a range of dance tutorials in different styles (hip-hop, contemporary, K-pop, etc.), along with helpful tips for dancers of varying skill levels.
  • Progress tracking tools: Allow users to monitor their dance skills, set goals, and track their improvement over time with built-in tools and progress reports.
  • Community engagement: Facilitate a space where users can share their routines, connect with others, ask for feedback, and participate in challenges.
  • Provide inspiration and motivation: Offer a motivational blog, highlight featured dancers, and host dance challenges to inspire members to improve.

Scenarios

Below are potential questions that users might ask when they visit the site:

  • How can I improve my flexibility and technique in contemporary dance? – Aimed at beginners or intermediate dancers looking for resources on specific dance styles.
  • What are the best K-pop routines for beginners to learn? – A question directed at beginners looking to start learning popular dance routines.
  • How can I track my progress and set goals for my dance practice? – A question focused on the tracking feature of the website.
  • Can I get feedback from other dancers on my routine? – A query related to the social aspects of the site, like sharing videos and receiving feedback.

Color Schema

The colors for the site are chosen to evoke energy, creativity, and enthusiasm, aligning with the vibrant nature of dance. These colors will be used strategically throughout the website:

  • Neon Pink (#FF00FF): Used for headings, call-to-action buttons, and hover effects to catch attention and encourage interaction.
  • Neon Teal (#00FFFF): Used for accent elements like links, buttons, and interactive elements to create contrast with the pink and balance the vibrancy.
  • Dark Gray (#222222): Used for the main body text to ensure readability and provide a neutral contrast against the bright background and accent colors.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for content backgrounds, spacing, and to create a clean, breathable design that prevents visual overload.

Typography

The typography has been selected to ensure readability and to evoke the energetic nature of the dance community. Here are the font choices:

  • Heading Font: Poppins (Google Fonts) – Used for all titles, headings, and section titles. It’s modern, clean, and easy to read.
  • Body Font: Roboto (Google Fonts) – Used for paragraphs, general content, and smaller text. It is neutral and highly readable on all screen sizes.
  • Accent Font: Raleway (Google Fonts) – Used for buttons and call-to-action links to add a touch of elegance and emphasis.
